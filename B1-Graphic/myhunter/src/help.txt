Welcome to a Duck-Hunt like game, based on the Pikmin theme,
you should help to fight Pikmin's enemies to save them all ! Good luck !

You have 3 lives which are represented by pikmin at the bottom in the middle of your screen,
But be careful, more you fight the enemies, more the difficulty increases.

Available imputs:
mouse left click.

Usage:
    ./my_hunter
        For launch the game.
    ./my_hunter -h
        For see this message.
