USAGE:
  ./my_defender
  In game, press 'q' to quit and 'Esc' to pause.
  Use either left or right click to interact with the game elements.

DESCRIPTION:
  This is a tower defense game.
  You must prevent the vehicles and soldier from reaching your base.
  When you have enough money, a tower's price will turn yellow.
  Drag and drop the tower in a valid spot to buy the tower.
  You lose when your health reaches 0.
  Enemies spawn every 30 seconds. Good luck!
